Three Paths, One Journey

Throughout this course, you'll notice content tailored to three different entrepreneur types. Explore each path below to find yours:

Choose Your Entrepreneur Path

Building SaaS products, apps, online platforms, or digital services. You're creating something that lives in the cloud.

Examples:
  • Software & Apps
  • Online Platforms
  • Digital Products
Common Challenges: Product-market fit, scaling infrastructure, customer acquisition costs
Key Weeks: Week 2 (Customer interviews for SaaS), Week 5 (Subscription pricing models)

Leveraging your knowledge and experience through consulting, coaching, or professional services.

Examples:
  • Consulting
  • Coaching & Training
  • Professional Services
Common Challenges: Pricing your expertise, scaling beyond time-for-money, building authority
Key Weeks: Week 4 (Relationship selling), Week 5 (Value-based pricing)

Creating physical products, retail experiences, or hospitality ventures. You're building something tangible.

Examples:
  • Physical Products
  • Retail & E-commerce
  • Food & Hospitality
Common Challenges: Inventory management, local market penetration, repeat customer loyalty
Key Weeks: Week 3 (MVP for physical products), Week 6 (Local marketing strategies)
